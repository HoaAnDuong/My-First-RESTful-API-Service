DATA VISUALIZATION

Here the documents of Data Visualization Tool.

Data Visualization is a field that people use graphic respresentation to analyse the data and imformation. Data Visualization is good for analyse the data, when it visualize the data,to make people easily to see the characteristics and trends of the data and help people make right decisions in business.

In this projects, i made a Data Visualisation tool by Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Tkinter. More details here: Introduction

The GUIs have two section: Field and Option. In the Field section, we have 3 Field: Revenue(Revenue Visualization), Storage(Storage Visualization), Tendency (Tendency Visualization). In the Option, we have Option corresponding to Field we selected(I selected Tendency Field):
